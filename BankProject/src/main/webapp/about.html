2. How to Securely Share Banking Information

Here are tips to share your banking details safely: ✅ Use encrypted communication: Send info over secure platforms (e.g., Signal, encrypted email).
✅ Share only what’s necessary: Avoid oversharing — account number and name might be enough.
❌ Avoid public channels: Never share on social media, forums, or unsecured websites.
✅ Use PDF with a password: If sending over email, consider encrypting your document.
✅ Verify the recipient: Double-check that you're sending it to the correct person or business.

3. How to Set Up a Bank Account

Here’s a general process:
🔹 Personal Account:
1. Choose a bank – Compare fees, services, online access.
2.Gather documents – Usually includes:
Government-issued ID (passport, driver’s license)
Proof of address (utility bill, lease agreement)
Social Security Number (in the US)
3.Apply online or in-branch
4.Deposit initial amount – Some banks require a minimum balance.
5.Activate account – You'll get a debit card, and online banking access.
🔹 Business Account:
Additional documents like:
Business registration
EIN (Employer Identification Number)
Articles of incorporation/partnership agreement
